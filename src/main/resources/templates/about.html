Our Story

Founded in 2020, Tab on Furniture began with a simple mission: to make high-quality furniture accessible to everyone. What started as a small online store has grown into a trusted furniture destination, serving customers worldwide.

We believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best without breaking the bank. Our curated collections feature the latest trends alongside timeless classics, ensuring that our customers always find exactly what they're looking for.
